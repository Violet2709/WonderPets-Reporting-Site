Interpretation

With an average SUS score of 72.6, the WonderPets prototype falls in the "Good" usability range. Scores above 68 are considered above average — WonderPets exceeds this benchmark.

This indicates most users found the system acceptable, easy to navigate, and would use it again — a strong result for a prototype-stage system.
