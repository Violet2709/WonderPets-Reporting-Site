Optional · Prototype Walkthrough

Walkthrough

A guided overview of the prototype's key screens and user flows evaluated during testing.

01
Screen 01

Landing Page

This is the landing page or homepage of a veterinary appointment website called WonderPets. The design is clean, simple, and focused on helping pet owners quickly understand the services offered and book appointments online.

⛶
02
Screen 02

Log In Page

The Log In feature of the WonderPets system allows registered users to securely access their personal accounts and use the platform's veterinary services online.

03
Screen 03

Dashboard

The dashboard page displays key information, recent activities, appointment updates, notifications, and quick access to important pet management features within the WonderPets system.

04
Screen 04

Book an Appointment

The appointment management page where users can view, schedule, and manage veterinary appointments for their pets quickly and conveniently.

05
Screen 05

Appointment Form

This page allows users to provide complete owner and pet information required for scheduling a veterinary appointment. It includes fields for personal details, pet characteristics, and appointment-related information to ensure accurate and organized booking.

06
Screen 06

Payment Method

This page allows users to choose their preferred payment option for the appointment, including bank transfer, e-wallet, or pay during visit, providing a flexible and convenient payment process.
